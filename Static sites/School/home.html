Our Curriculum

Pedagogy needs to evolve to keep pace with the changing needs of the 21stcentury. Pedagogy in India was shaped by the needs of the colonial powers and chiefly required recall of facts. Curricula were created to reflect that need. Pedagogy today requires the teacher to encourage students to create and communicate, reflect on, synthesise and apply knowledge. The NCERT has done significant work in this respect, However, no single curriculum has all the features that we require; hence we created our own, student centric curriculum that supports our education vision.

Blended Curriculum

We have taken of Montessori,Shri Ram Global School, Reggio Emilia, IGCSE and IB-PYP and integrated them into our curriculum. This includes a wider variety reading and literacy, problem solving and ability to synthesis the ideas. Hence, we have introduced global dimentions offering wide spectra of career shaping opportunity.

IIT Foundation

GVK offers a unique IIT, JEE, NEET in alliance with ALLEN DLP program foundation course for the students of grades 7th, 8th, 9th and 10th. the goal of this program is to make the students understand the very fundamentals of Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry and then learn them in a conceptual way.

Learning Beyond Classroom

GVK strongly believe that learning must extends beyond the classroom, in the spheres of art, craft, music & theatre. Such Activities helps children in nurturing talent, self-expression and enhancement of self-esteem.
